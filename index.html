    Welcome

    Our family owned and operated café/deli was established in November 2001. Inspired by the NY-style deli, but with a distinct European twist, it possesses a warm & inviting atmosphere.

    A great number of our delicious dishes have been passed down through generations and we believe that the nostalgic memories they evoke contribute to our success.
